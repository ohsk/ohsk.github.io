#30DayMapChallenge

Project Date
2022
Tools Used
UrbanFootprint, QGIS, OpenStreetMap, Aerialod, Blender, Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

A cartographic map series exploring Madison WI (and Dane County), following the 30 prompts for the month of November provided by @tjukanov, known as the #30DayMapChallenge on Twitter.
