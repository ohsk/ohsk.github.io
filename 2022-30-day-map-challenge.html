2022
Tools Used

Day 3

Polygons

Tweet thread

Wanted to see where #MissingMiddle housing types and density already exists in Madison. First filtered land use data from UrbanFootprint down to parcels w/ townhomes or that had 2-4 dwelling units. Then filtered out parcels that didn’t fall between 16-30 DUs/acres.

Important to note I didn’t do any assessment of actual housing affordability here. Not trying to imply that this existing housing stock shown solves for affordability or availability in Madison. I was mostly curious to see which areas already contained these housing types + density from a morphological perspective. There seems to be some concentration in Vilas, W Wash, along E Johnson, south of Willy St. And quite a spread around Atwood.
