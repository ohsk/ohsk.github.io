I'm an urban planner and designer working as a Design & Technology Fellow for the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau in Washington, D.C.

I am also a co-founder of the Urban Launchpad, a social venture dedicated to seeding and scaling urban data experiments in places that need it most.

Prior to switching to urban-scale projects, I cut my creative teeth designing lighting, products, packaging, soft goods, and websites.
