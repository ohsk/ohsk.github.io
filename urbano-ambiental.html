Urban Environmental Axis

Multisectoral urban intervention along the Choluteca River

Project Date
2014
Location
Tegucigalpa, Honduras
Role
Project Designer
Organization
Inter-American Development Bank

The IDB has proposed a model of comprehensive and multisectoral intervention to articulate urban development solutions in Tegucigalpa, developing a vision for the city built on a commitment to long-term investment.

The regeneration and urban densification the Comayagüela and Tegucigalpa urban centers builds on existing assets, environmentally, architecturally, and from socio-cultural heritage point of view.

This regeneration also seeks to revive private investment in the historic center, promote public safety, and encourage the development of new businesses and services.
